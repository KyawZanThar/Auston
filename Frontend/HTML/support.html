Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my password?

You can change your password by navigating to the "My Account" page in the sidebar and selecting "Change Password." Follow the on-screen instructions to update your password securely.

How can I access the digital library from home?

You can access the digital library by logging into your account with your school-provided credentials. Once logged in, you will have full access to all available audio books and resources.

How can I request a new audio book?

You can submit a request for a new audio book through the contact form on this page. Please provide the book's title and author, and we will do our best to add it to our library.

Can I download audio books for offline listening?

Yes, many of our audio books are available for download. After opening a book, you'll see a download option to save it to your device for offline use.

What should I do if a book is not loading or the audio is skipping?

First, please check your internet connection. If the issue persists, try clearing your browser's cache or using a different browser. If you still experience problems, please report the specific book and issue using our contact form.
